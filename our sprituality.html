Our Sprituality




PHJC Sprituality Our Spirituality: The spirituality of the Poor Handmaids of Jesus Christ is centered in Mary, the mother of Jesus. Mary was completely open to the invitation of God and wanted only to fulfil His will. Like her, we say “yes” with the gift of our lives.

Blessed Mary Katherine inspires us to be poor in the sense of our own nothingness and complete dependence on God’s providence.
